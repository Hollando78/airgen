The system shall have a Turret Subsystem that is capable of rotating 360 degrees.

The system shall have a Power Supply Subsystem that provides 24V DC to the Turret Subsystem.

## [DEF] Defensive Requirements

:::requirement{#SRD-DEF-001 title="SRD-DEF-001"}
The main battle tank SHALL be equipped with reactive armor capable of reducing the penetration of armor-piercing projectiles by at least 50%.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-DEF-002 title="SRD-DEF-002"}
The main battle tank SHALL have an active protection system that can intercept and neutralize incoming projectiles within a range of 30 meters.
:::

## [RED] Readiness

:::requirement{#SRD-RED-003 title="SRD-RED-003"}
The main battle tank SHALL be capable of operating with a minimum of 90% operational readiness rate under combat conditions.
:::

## [FUN] Functional Requirements

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-006 title="SRD-FUN-006"}
The main battle tank SHALL maintain a fuel efficiency of 2 kilometers per liter of diesel fuel when operating at a speed range of 10 to 60 kilometers per hour.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-007 title="SRD-FUN-007"}
The main battle tank SHALL be equipped with a fuel management system that accurately measures fuel consumption to within 1% accuracy.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-008 title="SRD-FUN-008"}
The main battle tank SHALL utilize diesel fuel with a density of 0.832 kg/L for the fuel efficiency measurement.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-009 title="SRD-FUN-009"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a fuel tank capacity of at least 100 liters to support the required operational range based on the fuel efficiency requirement.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-010 title="SRD-FUN-010"}
The main battle tank SHALL demonstrate a fuel efficiency of 2 kilometers per liter of diesel fuel during a continuous operation test lasting no less than 4 hours.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-011 title="SRD-FUN-011"}
The main battle tank SHALL achieve a minimum fuel efficiency of 2 kilometers per liter of diesel fuel under standard operating conditions as defined in the operational test plan.
:::

:::requirement{#SRD-FUN-014 title="SRD-FUN-014"}
The main battle tank SHALL provide real-time fuel efficiency data to the operator through the onboard display system.
:::

## [ENV] Environmental

:::requirement{#SRD-ENV-012 title="SRD-ENV-012"}
The main battle tank SHALL be capable of operating in temperatures ranging from -20°C to 50°C without affecting the fuel efficiency of 2 kilometers per liter.
:::

## [MTN] Maintainability

:::requirement{#SRD-MTN-013 title="SRD-MTN-013"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a maintenance schedule that includes checks on the fuel system components to ensure sustained fuel efficiency of 2 kilometers per liter.
:::

## [LGL] Legislation

:::requirement{#SRD-LGL-015 title="SRD-LGL-015"}
The main battle tank SHALL undergo fuel efficiency testing in accordance with military standard MIL-STD-810 to ensure compliance with the requirement.
:::

## [LETH] Lethality

:::requirement{#SRD-LETH-016 title="SRD-LETH-016"}
The system SHALL maintain an inventory of ammunition that includes at least 1000 rounds of 9mm cartridges, 500 rounds of 5.56mm cartridges, and 200 rounds of 7.62mm cartridges at all times to ensure operational readiness.
:::
