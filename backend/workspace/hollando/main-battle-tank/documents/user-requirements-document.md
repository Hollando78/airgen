# User Requirements Document

## [KEY] Key Requirements

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-001 title="URD-KEY-001"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a minimum fuel efficiency of 2 kilometers per liter of diesel fuel.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-002 title="URD-KEY-002"}
The main battle tank SHALL be capable of crossing a trench of at least 2.5 meters wide.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-003 title="URD-KEY-003"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a communication system that allows for secure voice communication over a distance of at least 20 kilometers.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-004 title="URD-KEY-004"}
The main battle tank SHALL be able to operate in temperatures ranging from -40 degrees Celsius to 50 degrees Celsius.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-005 title="URD-KEY-005"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a crew capacity of 4 personnel, including a commander, gunner, loader, and driver.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-006 title="URD-KEY-006"}
The main battle tank SHALL be equipped with a 120mm smoothbore cannon capable of firing at a rate of 10 rounds per minute.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-007 title="URD-KEY-007"}
The main battle tank SHALL be able to withstand impacts from 120mm armor-piercing rounds at a distance of 500 meters.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-008 title="URD-KEY-008"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a minimum operational range of 500 kilometers on a full tank of fuel.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-009 title="URD-KEY-009"}
The main battle tank SHALL be capable of achieving a maximum speed of 60 kilometers per hour on flat terrain.
:::

:::requirement{#URD-KEY-010 title="URD-KEY-010"}
The main battle tank SHALL have a maximum weight of no more than 70,000 kilograms.
:::
