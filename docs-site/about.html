About AIRGen

Modern requirements management for teams that build things that matter.

The Problem

Requirements management in regulated industries has been stuck for decades. Legacy tools like IBM DOORS cost hundreds of thousands of dollars, require dedicated administrators, and offer user experiences designed in the 1990s. Meanwhile, many teams resort to spreadsheets — losing traceability, version control, and any hope of compliance evidence.

Neither option works for modern engineering teams that need to move fast, maintain compliance, and actually enjoy their tools.

Our Approach

AIRGen combines three capabilities that have never existed together in a single platform:

  • AI-powered requirement generation — describe a need in plain language and get compliant requirement candidates in seconds, not hours of manual drafting
  • Deterministic quality assurance — every requirement is scored against ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148 checks with no AI hallucination, fully auditable and repeatable
  • Graph-based traceability — Neo4j stores every relationship between requirements, documents, architecture, and tests as first-class data, not afterthought annotations

The result is a platform that's faster to use than a spreadsheet and more capable than enterprise tools costing 100x more.

Built for Regulated Industries

AIRGen is purpose-built for teams working under regulatory constraints. Whether you're developing safety-critical automotive systems (ISO 26262), avionics software (DO-178C), medical devices (IEC 62304), or defense systems (CMMI, DO-178C), AIRGen provides the structure, traceability, and audit evidence your assessors require.

Automotive

ISO 26262 functional safety, ASPICE process compliance, tier-1 supplier workflows.

Aerospace

DO-178C software assurance, eVTOL certification, satellite systems, UAV development.

Medical Devices

IEC 62304 software lifecycle, FDA 510(k) submissions, SaMD classification support.

Defence

CMMI compliance, DO-178C for military avionics, self-hosted for security clearances.

Deployment Flexibility

We believe engineering teams should control where their data lives. AIRGen offers three deployment options:

  • SaaS — managed hosting at airgen.studio, zero infrastructure, start in minutes
  • Self-hosted — deploy with Docker on your own VPS or cloud for full data sovereignty
  • Managed hosting — we deploy and maintain AIRGen on your infrastructure with enterprise SLA

Self-hosted deployments run entirely on your infrastructure with no external data transfers, making AIRGen suitable for ITAR-controlled and export-restricted programmes.
